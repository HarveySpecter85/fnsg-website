# FAQs About Staffing Jobs and Temporary Employment

### What is a staffing job?
It is a role where a staffing agency hires you and places you with a client company, often for a project or limited time.

### Do temporary employees receive benefits?
Many South Fulton staffing agencies offer health, retirement, or PTO benefits once eligibility criteria are met.

### Can a temporary job turn into a permanent position?
Yes. Many South Fulton employers use temp-to-hire models to verify fit before offering full-time roles.

### How do I choose the right staffing agency?
Look for one experienced in your field, serving the South Fulton region, and clear about pay and expectations.

### What rights do temporary workers have?
You are protected by wage laws, overtime rules, safety regulations, and anti-discrimination laws even as a temp.
