## Navigating Permanent Placement Services: What to Expect

Permanent placement services help candidates find full time, long term roles while helping employers fill critical positions.

### The Process

1. **Initial Consultation:** Review skills, goals, salary needs, culture fit.  
2. **Matchmaking:** Recruiter identifies opportunities aligned with your profile.  
3. **Interview Prep:** Coaching, resume refinement, and expectations.  
4. **Offer Stage:** Recruiter negotiates salary and terms on your behalf.  

### What You Should Expect

- Personalized support  
- Transparency and honest feedback  
- Access to hidden job markets  
- Consistent communication

Important takeaway: The right permanent placement agency can shorten your job search and increase your chances of landing higher paying roles.
